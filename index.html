Our Story

About Viva La Vida Hotel

"Long Live" — the philosophy behind every moment at VlV.

Viva La Vida, taken from Spanish meaning "Long Live Life," is more than a name — it's our promise. Nestled in the vibrant heart of Valencia, Spain, Hotel VlV combines timeless elegance with modern luxury to create an experience that lingers long after checkout.

Renowned for our 4.9-star rating, we offer 250 meticulously appointed rooms, world-class dining at Vida Saludable, and an array of curated facilities designed to delight every guest.

15 years of exceptional hospitality in Valencia
Award-winning Vida Saludable restaurant
Complimentary breakfast across all room packages
24/7 personalised concierge service
Exclusive rooftop pool with panoramic city views

Vida Saludable

Our restaurant, named "Healthy Life," is a celebration of Spanish and Mediterranean cuisine. Set within a spacious, elegantly designed dining room, Vida Saludable serves fresh, seasonal ingredients transformed into memorable culinary experiences.

From the classic Tortilla Española at breakfast to slow-roasted Cordero Asado at dinner, every dish on our menu tells a story of Spain's rich culinary heritage.
